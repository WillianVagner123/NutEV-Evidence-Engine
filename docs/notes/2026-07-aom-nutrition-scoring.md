# AOM Nutrition Scoring Supplement

This note records the rationale for `config/scoring_rules_supplement_2026_07_aom_nutrition.json`.

## Scope

The supplement prioritizes records that explicitly connect anti-obesity medication, GLP-1 receptor agonists or incretin therapy with nutrition care, dietary counseling/counselling, adherence, lifestyle intervention, nutrition intervention and weight maintenance.

## Rationale

These records are increasingly relevant to NutMEV because they sit at the interface of obesity, cardiometabolic risk, lifestyle nutrition, adherence and implementation. The supplement does not broaden generic medication retrieval. It only adds points when medication terms are paired with nutrition, diet, adherence, lifestyle intervention or weight-maintenance language.

## Expected Effect

- `busca2a`: better prioritization of guidelines and consensus-style documents that include nutrition care around anti-obesity pharmacotherapy.
- `busca2b`: better prioritization of intervention and adherence studies around GLP-1/incretin therapy, diet support and post-treatment weight maintenance.
- Noise control: generic GLP-1 or medication-only records should not receive this bonus unless they include nutrition, diet, adherence, lifestyle intervention or maintenance terms.
