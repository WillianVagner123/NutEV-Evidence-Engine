# NutEV Conceptual Hierarchy

This document standardizes the language used in the thesis, articles, repository and platform.

## Hierarchy

```text
Field / area: Nutricao do Estilo de Vida
Central thesis product: Protocolo Dietetico NutEV
Visual representation: Piramide NutEV
Operational translation layer: Evidence-to-protocol workflow
Clinical-behavioral application layer: Framework comportamental / Nutricao Contingencial
Future measurement product: NutEV adherence index or questionnaire
```

## Definitions

### Nutricao do Estilo de Vida

The broader conceptual field aligned with Lifestyle Medicine. It integrates dietary patterns, nutritional quality, food processing, culinary skills, food literacy, food environment, adherence and clinical modifiers.

### Protocolo Dietetico NutEV

The central doctorate product. It translates evidence from food guidelines, clinical society documents and dietary-pattern evidence into candidate protocol components.

### Piramide NutEV

A visual synthesis of the NutEV protocol. It should not be created as an opinion-based figure. Each layer must be traceable to domains, claims, documents, evidence quality and human review decisions.

### Evidence-to-protocol workflow

The methodological system that links documents, evidence records, claims, evaluations, recommendation candidates, gaps, conflicts, human review and protocol readiness.

### Framework comportamental / Nutricao Contingencial

A complementary article/product that explains how NutEV can be applied clinically using behavior, context, contingencies and adherence. It is not the central thesis backbone during qualification.

### NutEV adherence index/questionnaire

A future product. It should not be required for qualification unless explicitly scoped as a later article.

## Recommended wording

Use:

```text
The NutEV dietary protocol is the central product of the thesis. The pyramid is a visual representation, and the behavioral framework is a complementary application model.
```

Avoid:

```text
NutEV is simultaneously a diet, a protocol, a framework, a pyramid and a questionnaire.
```

## Scope discipline

For qualification, keep the focus on:

1. evidence mapping and synthesis;
2. protocol development;
3. methodological traceability;
4. pilot-readiness.

Avoid expanding the core qualification scope to full validation of clinical effectiveness, questionnaire psychometrics, bariatric subgroups, GLP-1 subgroups or intervention trials unless these are explicitly framed as future studies.
