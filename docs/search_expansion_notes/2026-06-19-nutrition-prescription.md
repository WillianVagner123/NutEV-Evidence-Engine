# NutMEV search expansion: nutrition prescription

Date: 2026-06-19

## Rationale

This supplement adds a narrow semantic block for nutrition and dietary prescription terms. The goal is to improve retrieval of clinical nutrition pathways, dietary care protocols, and adherence-oriented intervention papers without broadening the query space into generic lifestyle content.

## Scope

Added terms target:

- nutrition prescription and dietary prescription variants;
- nutrition care protocol and dietary care pathway variants;
- adherence and implementation language tied specifically to prescribed dietary care.

## Expected effect

- Busca2a should retrieve more clinical guidance and consensus material where nutrition is framed as a prescribed therapeutic pathway.
- Busca2b should retrieve more adherence, counseling, implementation, and intervention studies tied to prescribed diet or nutrition plans.
- Noise risk is limited because broad terms like `prescription` are always anchored to nutrition, diet, dietary, care pathway, adherence, implementation, guideline, consensus, trial, or intervention context.
