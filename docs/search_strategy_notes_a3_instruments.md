# A3 instrument vocabulary supplement

This note documents the rationale for `config/keyword_taxonomy_supplement_a3_instruments.json`.

The supplement expands the Article 3 / A3 framework search layer for instrument-oriented evidence that is central to NutMEV but easy to miss with generic `questionnaire`, `instrument`, and `scale development` terms.

Added semantic blocks:

- dietary adherence instruments;
- culinary skills, cooking confidence, and cooking self-efficacy instruments;
- food competence and eating competence scales;
- commensality, shared meals, and family meals instruments.

Expected effect:

- improve retrieval of validation studies and psychometric instruments relevant to adherence, food literacy, culinary competence, and commensality;
- preserve precision by anchoring expansion to the A3/framework workstream and instrument-validation terms;
- preserve reproducibility by using the existing taxonomy supplement mechanism instead of editing or overwriting the base taxonomy.
